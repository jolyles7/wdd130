Overview

Purpose

Build Revenue by helping customers navigate rough waters by supplying them with the equipment and/or guides they need to safely head down the river.

Audience

Who are the Target Customers?

The target customers are young adults between 18-35 yrs old with interests in sporting.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

They expect to find supported locations, guide reviews, booking features, and rates.

How will my audience usually access my site?

They will access my site on their mobile phones.
